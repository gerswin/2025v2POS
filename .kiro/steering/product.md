# Venezuelan POS System

## Product Overview

Multi-tenant point-of-sale system specifically designed for event ticket sales in Venezuela with fiscal compliance. The system supports both general admission and numbered seating events with comprehensive pricing management.

## Key Features

- **Multi-tenant Architecture**: Complete data isolation between organizations using tenant-aware models
- **Event Management**: Venues, events, zones, and interactive seat maps
- **Flexible Pricing**: Dynamic pricing by zone, row, and individual seats with stage-based pricing
- **Ticket Sales**: Shopping cart, checkout process, and digital ticket generation with QR codes
- **Fiscal Compliance**: Venezuelan fiscal requirements with consecutive series numbering
- **Internationalization**: Spanish (primary) and English support
- **Payment Processing**: Full and partial payment support with installment plans
- **Notifications**: Email, SMS, and WhatsApp notifications for purchase confirmations and reminders

## Target Market

Venezuelan event organizers requiring fiscal compliance for ticket sales, supporting both small venues and large-scale events with complex seating arrangements.

## Business Model

B2B SaaS platform where each tenant (organization) manages their own events, customers, and sales independently with complete data isolation.
