    How Reconciliation Works
    1. Select Payment Method

    Choose the payment method you want to reconcile (e.g., Cash, Credit Card, Bank Transfer).

    2. Choose Date

    Select the date for which you want to reconcile payments. This will include all payments from 00:00 to 23:59 on that date.

    3. System Calculation

    The system will automatically calculate the total amount and count of transactions for the selected method and date.

    4. External Comparison

    After creation, you can enter external totals (from bank statements, card processor reports, etc.) to compare with system totals.

    Tip: Use the Preview button to see what payments will be included before creating the reconciliation.
    Important Notes
    • Only completed payments are included
    • Reconciliation covers full 24-hour period
    • Processing fees are included in totals
    • Refunded payments are excluded
    • Cannot reconcile future dates
